Rivers to Run

Hoback

Twelve miles south of Jackson you will find the Hoback River which is one of the more accessible rivers in the Jackson Hole. Highway 191 parallels it the river most of the Hoback's length from the confluence with the Snake River to where the river turns west into the Wyoming Range.

Yellowstone River

Gardiner River

Rising on the northeast slope of Joseph Peak in the northwest corner of Yellowstone Park the Gardiner River is born. The river starts at 10,000 feet. By the time it reaches the northwest corner of Gardner's Hole it has been joined by several other icy small streams.
